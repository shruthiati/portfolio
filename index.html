About Me

I'm an Operations Performance Analyst specializing in healthcare revenue cycle management. I hold a degree in Health Science with a minor in Computer Science from Texas State University and am currently pursuing a master's in IT Management at Cumberland University. My academic background allows me to bridge the gap between healthcare operations, technology, and data analytics.

I have always been passionate about making a change in healthcare. When I first learned about the relationship between healthcare expenses and the quality of care in the U.S., I realized how complex our current system is. I understand that untangling this complexity and creating meaningful solutions will take years, but I am committed to being part of that transformation.

My Expertise
I work across the front, middle, and back-end of RCM, with a strong understanding of Medicare, Medicaid, and managed care payers. I leverage technologies like EPIC, FinThrive, Waystar, ePremis, ACE, and MicroStrategy to drive data-backed operational improvements and financial performance.

My Approach & Goals
I believe that healthcare revenue cycle management needs more technological innovation, particularly in business insights and data-driven decision-making. My master's in IT Management is equipping me with the skills to help RCM companies leverage data analytics for smarter, more efficient operations. My long-term goal is to become an operations performance manager, leading initiatives that optimize revenue cycle processes through technology and strategic insights—ultimately improving financial efficiency without compromising care quality
